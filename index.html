CONCEPT

The concept behind Projits Solutions is simple. It identifies a unit of IT work as a ProjIT, which is just a way of saying "IT Project" or "Project of IT". A Projit is defined here as a task or collective tasks which result in an output of a deliverable. It can be small, for example, writing a simple Program to produce a game. Or it can be a bigger task, such as building a customized web application for a company, for example, Studygroops.com. Projit Solutions offers complete end-to-end management and delivery of IT related projects for all industries.

VISION

The vision of Projits Solutions is to integrate Technology solutions into various companies in different disciplines. An example showcase is the Projit for the Education industry, StudyGroops (please refer to the PROJITS tab for more info).

The StudyGroops application was formed from a simple use case requirement specification: the goal of the application. Projits Solutions welcomes the opportunity to work with different industries to implement useful IT services to the organizations by identifying the goals and helping them achieve them with the use of Technology.
